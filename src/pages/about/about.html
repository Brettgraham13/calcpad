Canvas Rules

Our canvas is an arithmetic solver where input equations can be solved under the following conditions:

  • The equation must contain numbers and/or fractions and at least one operator from the list below

Basic Algebra

Draw mathematical expressions on the canvas, and press the equals button in order to calculate them.

Equation Solver

The canvas can solve equations with one unknown, with the variable to be calculated written as a question mark. Scribble over an equation to clear it!

Functions

A wide range of advanced calculation options are available, such as trigonometric functions and logarithms.

Calculator Rules

A traditional calculator is also included in CalcPad, which has the following guidelines:

  • Invalid strings are not evaluated and will give an error message describing the error
  • Numbers larger than 2^31 - 1 may be rounded and are accompanied by a warning message
  • Numbers larger than 10^15 - 1 are expressed in scientific notation
  • Exponents are denoted by putting two multiplication symbols in a row with the exponent wrapped in parentheses
  • Missing parentheses at the end of a string are autofilled, and a confirmation message checks that the appropriate string was generated
  • Strings larger than 15 characters are not handled
